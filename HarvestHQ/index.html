    How do I register on HarvestHQ?

    • To register on HarvestHQ, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in the required information, and follow the on-screen instructions. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

    What payment methods are supported on HarvestHQ?

    • HarvestHQ currently supports payments through various methods, including credit/debit cards, online banking, and mobile wallets.
    • Specific details on supported payment providers can be found in the payment section of your account settings.

    How does HarvestHQ ensure the security of my data?

    • At HarvestHQ, we prioritize the security and privacy of your data.
    • We implement robust encryption protocols, conduct regular security audits, and adhere to industry best practices.
    • For more details on our security measures, please refer to our Security Policy in the Help Center.
